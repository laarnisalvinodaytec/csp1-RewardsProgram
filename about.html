About Us

Our company offers a rewards/loyalty program that may give a customer/employee free merchandise, rewards, coupons, or even advance released products.

With a team that possesses skills in website development, merchandising and merchandise fulfillment – we work with you in running a performance based rewards program that helps increase your team’s productivity and motivation.

Our company adopts the following systems:

  1. POINT SYSTEM

    2. Frequent customers earn points, which translate into some type of reward. Whether it’s a discount, a freebie, or special customer treatment, customers work toward a certain amount of points to redeem their reward.

  2. TIER SYSTEM

    3. Implementing a tiered system would balance attainable and desirable rewards and encourages more purchases. Present small rewards as a base offering for being a part of the program, and then encourage repeat customers by increasing the value of the rewards as the customer moves up the loyalty ladder. This helps solve the problem of members forgetting about their points and never redeeming them because the time between purchase and gratification is too long.
